Embedded Device Developer (Summer Intern)

Summer of 2015 - 2017
nquiringminds, Southampton
2015

I designed and developed a set of IoT (Internet of Things) sensors, including temperature, humidity, carbon monoxide, combustible gas and sound sensors, that were deployed to several industrial settlements. The development process involved research and sourcing of electronic components, circuit design, PCB drawing, 3D CAD modelling and mechanical desing of the cases.

Moreover, I wrote the software for the sensors according to the company's requirements as well as the backend functionality needed to communicate with the IoT platform.

2016

I reverse engineered Heiman's Home Automation Kit and integrated the sensors into our IoT platform. I also developed suitable frontend and backend applications using Javascript and NodeJS. Sample data that has been collected with these sensors can be found in nquiringminds' TDX (Trusted Data Exchange).

2017

I was involved in the Graincare project funded by Innovate UK. Together with one other intern and members of Brunel University, we developed an IoT solution and framework to measure different aspects of grain storages in India to help prevent rotting.
