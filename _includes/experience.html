3rd Year Individual Project

2016 - 2017
University of Southampton, Southampton

My project's title was Linear Quadratic Regulation using Reinforcement Learning and its main objective was to apply reinforcement learning algorithms to balance an inverted pendulum with one of its ends attached to a controllable cart. Algorithms and concepts such as the Bellman Equations, Temporal Difference Learning and Adaptive Q-Learning were implemented. The theoretical research was mainly based on the "Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction" book by Sutton and Barto, as well as the series of Reinforcement Learning lectures presented by David Silver, from Google Deepmind.
